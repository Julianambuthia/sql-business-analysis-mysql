# Business Insights

## Revenue Analysis

Technology products are the primary driver of revenue, indicating strong demand in this category.

## Product Performance

Furniture includes multiple loss-making products, suggesting pricing or cost inefficiencies. Technology products show consistently positive profitability.

## Customer Behavior

Revenue is concentrated among a small group of customers, highlighting the importance of retaining high-value customers.

## Regional Performance

The West and East regions generate the highest sales, indicating stronger market performance compared to other regions.

## Trends Over Time

Sales vary over time, suggesting potential seasonality or fluctuations in demand that could be further analyzed with larger datasets.
